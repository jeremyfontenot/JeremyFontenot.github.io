Monitoring and Logging Strategy

Back to Home Lab Documentation Hub

Observability Goals

  • Detect service degradation before user-impacting failure.
  • Correlate infrastructure, security, and application events.
  • Retain enough telemetry for incident reconstruction and trend review.

Platform Roles

  • Zabbix: system health, uptime, and threshold-based alerts.
  • Wazuh: endpoint security events and policy compliance.
  • Graylog: centralized logging, dashboarding, and search.

Operational Review Cadence

  • Daily: critical alerts and service availability checks.
  • Weekly: trend review and recurring event analysis.
  • Monthly: tuning of thresholds, false-positive reduction, retention checks.
