Recovery Objectives (Target)

  • Identity core services: RTO 4 hours, RPO 24 hours
  • Monitoring stack: RTO 8 hours, RPO 24 hours
  • Non-critical workloads: RTO 24 hours, RPO 48 hours

Backup Scope

  • VM snapshots and configuration exports.
  • Critical configuration backups (firewall, AD metadata, monitoring configs).
  • Documentation and change records in source-controlled copies.

Recovery Sequence

  • Recover virtualization host and storage availability first.
  • Restore AD and DNS services to stabilize identity dependencies.
  • Recover firewall/routing policies and validate segmentation.
  • Restore monitoring stack and confirm alert pipeline integrity.

Testing Cadence

  • Quarterly partial restore drills.
  • Semi-annual full recovery simulation and runbook updates.
