Validation & Audit

This page documents the lab setup, automation, validation steps, and artifacts for Validation & Audit.

Validation

  • Expected outputs: CSVs, logs, portal screenshots
  • Audit: Unified Audit Log queries and CA evaluation results

Rollback

  • Remove test users/groups with filters (LAB-*)
  • Disable or delete lab policies

Artifacts

  • Reports (CSV/HTML/PDF)
  • Screenshots (annotated)
  • Change records
  • Scripts and configs
