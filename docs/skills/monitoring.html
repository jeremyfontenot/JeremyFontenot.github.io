Monitoring & Security Visibility

Full-stack monitoring and SIEM visibility using Wazuh, Zabbix, and Graylog. Includes log ingestion, alerting, and operational dashboards.

The objective is early detection and clear triage. Monitoring data is used to confirm service status, spot drift, and support incident response decisions with evidence from logs, dashboards, and alert history.
