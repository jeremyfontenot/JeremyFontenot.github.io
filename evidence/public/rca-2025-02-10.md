Summary

This redacted public copy summarizes the root cause analysis of a mail flow outage. Sensitive identifiers and ticket numbers are redacted.

Root Cause

A transport rule introduced a routing loop under specific message headers.

Actions Taken

- Rolled back the recent change and implemented a remediation script.
