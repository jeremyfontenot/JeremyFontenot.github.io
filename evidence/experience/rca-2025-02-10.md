# RCA: Mail Flow Outage — 2025-02-10

Summary

On 2025-02-10 an outbound mail-flow outage impacted ~12% of users in a multi-tenant environment. This RCA documents the incident timeline, root cause, remediation, and long-term mitigations.

Root Cause

A recently-deployed transport rule (ID: 7f3a) contained a misconfigured condition that introduced a routing loop under specific message headers.

Actions Taken

- Isolated impacted tenant routing and disabled transport rule 7f3a.  
- Rolled back the recent change and revalidated MX routing.  
- Created `Remediation-DisableTransportRule.ps1` to automate safe rollback.  

Outcome

- Service restored in 2.4 hours.  
- Post-incident monitoring showed no recurrence for the following 9 months.
