About Me

Hi, I’m Jeremy Fontenot, an IT professional with expertise in Service Desk Support, Systems Administration, and Networking. With a strong background in Active Directory, Microsoft 365, Azure, and network security, I specialize in troubleshooting, system optimization, and IT infrastructure management.

I hold an A.S. in Computer Science - Database Administration and completed the Information Security and Administration Program with a 4.00 GPA. My certifications include CompTIA A+, Server+, ITF+, Microsoft Azure Fundamentals, MTA (Windows Server, Networking, Security), Linux Essentials, and Google IT Support.

Beyond my professional work, I’ve built an enterprise-grade home lab, featuring a Dell R710 virtualization host running ESXi for Active Directory, web hosting, and security monitoring, with secure networking, dynamic DNS, and VPN solutions.

I’m passionate about technology, security, and automation, always exploring new tools and refining my skills to solve complex IT challenges. Whether it’s troubleshooting an issue, designing a network, or optimizing systems, I strive to deliver efficient, practical solutions.

Feel free to connect with me or explore my projects to learn more!
