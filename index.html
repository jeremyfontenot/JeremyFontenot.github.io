About Me

Hi, I’m Jeremy Fontenot — a cloud-first IT professional focused on Microsoft 365 administration, identity management, and modern workplace automation. I specialize in designing secure, efficient environments using Entra ID, Intune, Exchange Online, SharePoint, and the Power Platform.

I hold an A.S. in Computer Science – Database Administration and completed the Information Security and Administration Program with a 4.00 GPA. My certifications span CompTIA A+, Server+, ITF+, Microsoft Azure Fundamentals, MTA (Windows Server, Networking, Security), Linux Essentials, and Google IT Support.

Outside of work, I maintain a personal Microsoft 365 cloud lab where I design, secure, and automate modern identity and collaboration solutions — from Entra Conditional Access and Intune compliance policies to Exchange mail security and Power Automate workflows. All runbooks and architecture docs are versioned in SharePoint.

I’m driven by technology, security, and automation — constantly exploring cloud-first approaches to tackle real-world IT challenges. Whether it’s tightening identity access, building automated workflows, or hardening an M365 tenant, I prioritize practical, secure outcomes.
